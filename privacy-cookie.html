Privacy & Cookies

Mindjset uses cookies and similar technologies only after your consent. These tools help us understand audience behaviour, improve the website experience, and measure the performance of campaigns and ticket drops.

Tools in use

  • Meta Pixel for campaign performance and remarketing analysis
  • Microsoft Clarity for anonymous session insights and UX improvements

When they activate

Tracking scripts are loaded only after the user clicks Accept in the cookie banner. If the user clicks Reject, no marketing or analytics tracking is activated.

How to change your choice

To update your cookie preference, clear your browser local storage for this site and reload the page. The consent banner will appear again and you can make a new choice.
